15 Years Experiance

Root Coir is a long-standing family business with a focus on coconut products. Based in Pollachi, Southern India, known as the "Coconut City," our operations benefit from the region's tropical climate and abundant rainfall, fostering lush coconut tree growth. Our freshwater irrigation system ensures optimal conditions for coconut growth, naturally maintaining the pH and EC levels essential for our products without artificial processes.

Our modern infrastructure boasts cutting-edge machinery, facilitating efficient production and post-production processes. With dedicated sections for manufacturing, quality control, storage, and packaging, we ensure timely completion of all operations. Supported by a robust client base, we effectively meet the diverse needs of our customers.

Headquartered in London, United Kingdom, our main office serves our UK and European clientele, offering personalized service through face-to-face interactions. At Root Coir, our commitment to service and satisfaction drives our business forward, with happy customers forming the cornerstone of our success.

For over two decades, Root Coir has been delivering high-quality Coir products to more than 12 countries. As a traditional family-run business, we have specialized in sustainable manufacturing and exporting of Coir products.

Our commitment to sustainability is reflected in our production processes, which are designed to minimize our environmental impact while producing superior quality products. Our range of Coir Pith and Grow Bags are highly sought after for their eco-friendliness, durability, and effectiveness.
